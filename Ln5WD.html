PRE.DO at Nibe Festival

Nibe Festival works with innovation all year long, which is why they have chosen PRE.DO: to capture the volunteers’ ideas and implement the best ones during the upcoming festival.

With more than 4,000 volunteers, it is not hard to overlook good ideas. In the video here, you can listen to Thomas Bruun talking  (Danish) about how PRE.DO makes a difference in the innovation process of Nibe Festival, one of Denmark’s top 10 biggest festivals.

Are you interested in hearing more about how PRE.DO helps innovate and capture ideas? Then sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know about our events, news and collaborations.

Thomas Bruun, B2B Sales and Marketing Manager, Nibe Festival
